# MyClassboard

## Overview
MyClassboard is one of India's premier school management software solutions, serving 5,000+ schools with a comprehensive feature set. It offers native mobile apps for parents, teachers, and students, along with WhatsApp integration — setting the bar for parent communication.

## Feature List
- Student Information System (SIS)
- Attendance (daily & period-wise)
- Fee management
- Exam management & report cards
- Transport management
- Library management
- Hostel management
- Alumni management
- Inventory management
- Certificate generation
- Student profile with digital document library
- School services & SQAA
- Dynamic forms & automated alerts
- Parent, teacher, and student mobile apps
- WhatsApp integration

## Pricing
- **₹18,000+/year** — moderate for mid-sized schools

## Strengths
- Comprehensive feature set covering almost all school operations
- Native mobile apps for **all three stakeholders** (parent, teacher, student) — best-in-class
- WhatsApp integration for parent communication
- Digital document library for student records
- Long market presence and brand trust in India
- SQAA (School Quality Assessment & Assurance) support

## Weaknesses
- Pricing is moderate — not competitive for budget schools
- UI/UX could be modernized (feels legacy)
- Not AI-forward — no AI question formatting or predictive analytics
- No public website CMS bundled
- No freemium or low-cost tier

## What SNR Can Learn
1. **Mobile apps for all stakeholders** — MyClassboard has three native apps; this is the gold standard
2. **WhatsApp integration is now table-stakes** — parents expect communication via WhatsApp
3. **Digital document library** — paperless student records are a major selling point
4. **SQAA compliance** — demonstrates deep understanding of Indian school accreditation
5. **Hostel & alumni modules** — schools with boarding need these
