# Entab / CampusCare

## Overview
Entab/CampusCare has been serving the Indian school ERP market for 23+ years, with 2,500+ schools across 28 states. Their flagship product, CampusCare 10X, positions itself as "the most advanced school management software." They uniquely bundle ERP, LMS, digital content, and website into an integrated offering, with explicit alignment to India's NEP 2020.

## Feature List
- School ERP (SIS, attendance, fees, exams, timetable)
- Learning Management System (LMS)
- Digital content library (HD quality)
- Smart lesson plans aligned to curriculum
- AI-based assessments & practice worksheets
- Public website builder
- Mobile apps
- NEP 2020 aligned experiential learning
- 4 product lines: ERP, LMS, Digital Content, Website

## Pricing
- **Custom pricing** (not publicly disclosed) — tailored per school based on size and modules
- Generally considered premium due to bundled digital content

## Strengths
- **Deep India presence** — 2,500+ schools, 28 states, 400+ staff
- **LMS + Digital Content bundling** — unique differentiator; schools get ready-made digital lessons
- **NEP 2020 alignment** — explicitly designed around India's new education policy
- **23 years of trust** — well-established brand in Indian education
- **Large support team** — 400+ staff ensures on-ground support
- **AI-based assessments** — practice worksheets with auto-evaluation
- **Experiential learning** — NEP-aligned pedagogy tools

## Weaknesses
- **Custom pricing** — lack of transparency makes it hard for small schools to evaluate
- **Legacy technology** — perceived as older stack, slower innovation cycle
- **Expensive support** — large team cost passed to schools
- **Slow UI modernization** — not as polished as newer SaaS tools
- **No transparent API** — closed ecosystem limits integrations

## What SNR Can Learn
1. **LMS + Content bundling is a winning strategy** — schools love getting digital lessons with their ERP
2. **NEP 2020 alignment is essential** — for India market, this is a checkbox requirement
3. **AI-based assessments** — auto-evaluated practice worksheets reduce teacher workload
4. **Experiential learning** — moving beyond admin to pedagogy creates differentiation
5. **400+ staff model** — shows the level of support needed for enterprise school sales
