# Fedena

## Overview
Fedena is the largest open-source school management system globally, with 40,000+ schools across 200+ countries in 20+ languages. Built on Ruby on Rails, it offers both a free community edition and a paid enterprise version. Acquired by Practically (2024–2025), it remains the feature-breadth benchmark with 100+ modules.

## Feature List
- SIS, attendance, fees, timetable, exams, library, transport
- HR management, payroll
- Alumni management
- Built-in LMS
- Multi-campus support
- 20+ language translations
- 100+ modules covering every school operation
- 24/7 enterprise support
- Self-hosted (community) or cloud (enterprise)

## Pricing
- **Community Edition:** Free (self-hosted, open source)
- **Enterprise Cloud:** ₹15,000+/year (custom pricing)
- Significant infrastructure and maintenance costs for self-hosted

## Strengths
- MASSIVE module breadth — 100+ modules surpass every competitor
- 20+ languages — true global readiness
- Open-source community edition drives adoption and word-of-mouth
- Mature product with years of production hardening
- 24/7 support for enterprise customers
- Multi-campus support
- Recently acquired by Practically (financial backing)

## Weaknesses
- Monolithic Rails application — modernization is slow
- Not cloud-native (Firestore, serverless, etc.)
- Self-hosted operations burden for community edition
- UI feels dated compared to modern SaaS products
- No AI features (question formatting, analytics)
- No native public website CMS
- ID card generation is basic

## What SNR Can Learn
1. **Module breadth is the gold standard** — Fedena proves schools want depth (100+ modules)
2. **Open-source drives adoption** — community edition creates a pipeline of paying enterprise customers
3. **Multi-language is essential** — 20+ languages opens global markets
4. **Alumni module** — long-term value beyond graduation
5. **HR/Payroll** — schools want staff management in the same platform
