About Apex Public School

Quality Education with Discipline Since 2018

Welcome to Apex Public School

Apex Public School, located in Anjani Bazar, Parsa, Saran, is committed to providing quality education from Play Group to Class 8. We focus on academic excellence, moral values, discipline, and overall personality development of every child in a safe and modern environment.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading school known for academic excellence, discipline, and character building.

Mission

To provide affordable quality education and develop responsible, disciplined, and confident students ready for future challenges.

Principal's Message

Education builds confidence, discipline and strong character. At Apex Public School, we believe every child deserves quality education, especially those from economically weaker families.

Our dedicated teachers focus on strong academic foundation, moral values, and preparation for future competitive examinations. We create a safe, loving, and modern learning environment where every student can grow into a responsible and successful citizen.

— Baby Kumari
Principal, Apex Public School
